never again.

a reflection on America's growing gun culture.

This booklet compiles articles on the US school shooting culture. It explores the gun culture that American children are susceptible to and how it affects their everyday life. The two events chosen were the Columbine High School shooting on April 20, 1999 and the Stoneman High School shooting on February 12, 2018.


This work is part of the project "Reality Box”.
