clean air campus

As part of the goals for 2019/20 academic year, Cardiff Met wants to be the first Welsh university to ban smoking and to be able to say they have a “clean air” campus. We were given the task to develop a campaign that would communicate the new policy.


With that in mind, we developed a series of posters to be hanged in the main entrances of campus in order to inform, as asked by the client.


I developed an app that compiles all the information that students, staff, and visitors need to know about this policy.
