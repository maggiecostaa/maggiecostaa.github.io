freedom?

hello, have you met America?

“Freedom?” is a multi-volume magazine that aims to inform the public about amerciaʼs contemporary culture. The base ground is the question “Why is America the greatest coun- try in the world?”, asked and answered in the first episode of the series “The Newsroom”.


Using speeches by important and mediator Americans, the reader is changelled and sometimes provoked to understand a bit more subjects such as, American constitution, gun control and the source of their violence. On overall it question American freedom, the thing Americans are most proud of. It does not intend to force a change in ones idea of America, just strip it from its idealisms.
